Our story

Wellness stopped making sense to us as separate rooms.

Meridian started in 2019 when a physician, a strength coach and a yoga teacher shared an office by accident — and noticed how much better their patients and clients did when the three of them actually talked.

What we believe

Four principles, one file on you.

01

Continuity beats intensity

A moderate practice you can sustain for years outperforms an aggressive one you abandon in six weeks.

02

Data should travel with you

Your trainer, dietitian and physician read the same notes — nothing gets re-explained from scratch.

03

The body is one system

Sleep, stress, movement and food are never treated as unrelated variables here.

04

Small studios, real relationships

Six locations, capped class sizes — we'd rather grow slowly than lose the thing that works.

Built by the people who use it.

Every member of our founding team still teaches, trains or sees patients weekly.
