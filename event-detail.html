Sunrise Breathwork

A 45-minute guided breathwork session to open the week — open to members and first-time visitors alike.

What to bring

Just yourself and a mat if you have one — we keep a stock on hand. Sessions run rain or shine, indoors in Studio A.

Led by

Our meditation lead guides each session through a short warm-up, a structured breathing sequence, and ten minutes of guided stillness to close.

Most first-timers say the hardest part is showing up at 6am. The rest takes care of itself.
