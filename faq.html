Frequently asked

Questions, answered plainly.

Do I need to choose one program to start?
No — most members start with whichever program solves their most immediate need, then add others once the intake team has a fuller picture. See all programs.
Does membership include physician visits?
Continuous and Complete Care plans include quarterly or monthly consults respectively. Foundation members can book consults separately. See membership plans.
Do you take insurance?
We accept most major insurance for physician and dietitian consults; studio classes are billed through membership only. Contact us with your provider details and we'll confirm coverage before you book.
Can I try a class before joining?
Yes — every new visitor gets one complimentary class in any program. Book your first visit.
How does information move between my coach and my doctor?
Everyone on your care team works from the same shared notes, updated after each session or consult — you never have to relay your own history between departments.
Can I pause or cancel my membership?
Yes, anytime from your account or by contacting our front desk — there's no lock-in contract on any plan.

Still have a question?

We usually reply within one business day.
