Physician Consultation

A 30-minute, one-on-one consult with an internist or sports-medicine physician who already has your training, sleep and nutrition history in front of them.

Book a consultation All services

What to expect

Most members book their first consult after a few weeks in a program — often prompted by a coach or dietitian who flags something worth a physician's eyes. You'll meet in a private room at your home studio, or by video if that's easier.

Because your physician can already see your training volume, sleep trends and meal plans, the conversation starts well past "so, tell me about yourself." Expect it to focus on the specific thing that brought you in — a nagging joint, a plateau, a lab result worth discussing.

After the visit

Notes are shared with the rest of your care team the same day, so your next training or nutrition session already reflects anything that changed. No forms to re-fill out, no history to repeat.

The goal isn't more appointments. It's fewer, better ones.

Who this is for

  • Members returning from injury who want a coordinated return-to-training plan
  • Anyone due for an annual physical or bloodwork review
  • Members whose coach or dietitian has recommended a check-in
